An electric bicycle (e-bike, eBike, etc.) is a motorized
bicycle with an integrated electric motor used to assist
propulsion.


About Electric Cycle

Electric cycles (or e-bikes as they are often known) are the more inclusive, more versatile version of the regular bicycle. They look the same as a normal bicycle and can be ridden the same way, but they have a battery and a small electric motor which makes it easier to use the pedals.
